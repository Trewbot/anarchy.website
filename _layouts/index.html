Anarchy.Website

Welcome to anarchy.website, a collection of written works by Una J. Ada, a.k.a. Trevor (J.) Hoglund* or Trewbot. Areas of interest and thus common topics on this site include but are not limited to: absurdism, anarchism, comedy, consciousness, direct action, discrete mathematics, functional programming, futurism, linguistics, machine learning, magic, prefigurative politics, sociology, symbology, technogaianism, transhumanism, quantum physics. All works are available under a Creative Commons Attribution Share-Alike 4.0 International License except where otherwise noted.

For works in other media, including videos, photography, and programming by Una, refer to trew.moe.

* While I would like to eventually use only names that I have chosen for myself rather than those that were assigned to me, I would also like this site to serve as a proper archive of works I have written. I take no offense to being reffered to by any name not intended as offensive that is used to refer to me, I view any name used to refer to something or someone as a valid name for that thing or person. As such, works are saved here under the name that they were originally written/published under.
